# ABOMINATION: OCCULT WARFARE & TACTICAL COMBAT MANUAL

This manual provides an exhaustive accounting of the real-time tactical combat engine in **Abomination**. It details the structural specifications of every unit, vehicle, and alchemical ordnance card, maps out the command leadership profiles, and outlines the philosophical and mechanical distinctions of the nine secret society factions.

## 1. SECRET SOCIETY FACTIONS

In the subterranean struggle for alchemical supremacy and political hegemony across 19th-century Europe, nine esoteric secret societies vie for influence. Each faction operates under a distinct tactical doctrine, possessing an exclusive Master Commander and two specialized Combat Cards.

### I. The Freemasons (Architects of Order & Geometry)
* **Philosophy**: Dedicated to the preservation of absolute civic order, structural unassailability, and the mathematical perfection of sacred geometry.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Heavy siege engineering and rapid structural fortification. Masonic forces excel at bypassing enemy foot soldiers to dismantle defensive towers and accelerating friendly troop movements across the grid.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Grand Master Hiram** (Generates continuous alchemical self-healing and fortifies nearby defensive architecture).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Masonic Sapper` (Siege Squad: Bypasses infantry to target defensive towers exclusively with heavy demolition charges).
  2. `Sacred Geometry` (Support Blessing: Instantly imbues all friendly units across the field with +40% tactical movement velocity).

### II. The Rosicrucians (Alchemical Occultists & Mystics)
* **Philosophy**: Hermetic alchemists seeking the ultimate transmutation of the human soul and the mastery of biological corruption and restoration.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Unyielding biological attrition. They deploy massive, high-vitality flesh constructs to absorb enemy fire while purging allied debuffs with restorative elixirs.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Magus Rosenkreuz** (Wields esoteric toxic attacks that corrupt enemy life force while directing occult constructs).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Homunculus Behemoth` (Tank Squad: Possesses colossal structural vitality [800 HP] and absolute poison immunity, acting as an unyielding meat-shield with virtually zero offensive attack).
  2. `Elixir of Vitality` (Support Ordnance: Engulfs allied forces in a concentrated restorative mist that instantly heals structural vitality).

### III. The Knights Templar (Relic Keepers & Holy Warriors)
* **Philosophy**: An ancient military priorate sworn to protect holy relics and cleanse occult heresy through uncompromising holy fire.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Pyromancy and area denial. Templar units wield flaming greatswords and volatile incendiary grenades that engulf the grid in unquenchable, spreading fire.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Grand Prior Jacques** (An armored zealot prior possessing supreme physical defense and absolute immunity to catching fire).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Templar Pyre Knight` (Melee Squad: Elite samurai-kabuto shock troops whose flaming greatswords ignite targets with persistent fire DOT).
  2. `Greek Fire Flask` (Support Ordnance: Hurls a volatile incendiary flask that ignites a wide area in unquenchable, spreading flames).

### IV. Gnomes of Zurich (Gold Hoarders & Financial Oligarchs)
* **Philosophy**: Subterranean banking syndicates who believe absolute political power flows entirely from the accumulation of capital and debt leverage.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Decapitation strikes and financial bounty. They deploy specialized vault assassins to neutralize rival leadership while deploying debt collectors who yield financial rewards even in death.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **High Banker Rothschild** (Directs elite headhunters and subterranean financial syndicates from behind armored frontlines).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Vault Assassin` (Ranged Squad: Bypasses ordinary troops and towers entirely to target rival commanders exclusively with high-caliber sniper rifles).
  2. `Zurich Debt Collector` (Melee Squad: Syndicate enforcers who execute an on-death trigger: dropping a +50 CHF financial bounty upon death).

### V. The Carbonari (Charcoal Burners & Revolutionary Conspirators)
* **Philosophy**: Radical fraternal networks of charcoal burners and republican conspirators dedicated to the fiery overthrow of monarchial tyranny.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Incendiary bombardment and explosive martyrdom. Carbonari skirmishers lob burning pitch-bombs, while fanatic martyrs carry concealed explosives into enemy ranks.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Grand Master Alta Vendita** (Revolutionary agitator whose standard attacks inflict severe incendiary damage over time).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Carbonari Arsonist` (Ranged Squad: Hurls flaming charcoal pitch-bombs over enemy frontlines to engulf regiments in spreading fire).
  2. `Revolutionary Martyr` (Melee Squad: Fanatic insurgents who execute an on-death trigger: detonating a devastating 45ft radius shrapnel blast [150 Dmg] upon death).

### VI. The Golden Dawn (Hermetic Magicians & Astral Travelers)
* **Philosophy**: An occult hermetic order dedicated to the practical mastery of ceremonial magic, astral plane navigation, and subverting human free will.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Psychological subversion and hypnotic mind control. They beguile enemy foot soldiers onto the player's side and turn strongest rival units against their own masters.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Imperator Crowley** (Occult ceremonial master possessing complete magical immunity and long-range astral bolt projection).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Hermetic Mesmerist` (Caster Squad: Occult hypnotists who beguile an enemy squad member under your command for a brief duration upon summoning).
  2. `Astral Hypnosis` (Support Ordnance: Casts an overwhelming hypnotic pendulum overlay that instantly seizes total mind control of the strongest enemy unit for 12 seconds).

### VII. Fenian Brotherhood (Radical Insurgents & Night Raiders)
* **Philosophy**: Clandestine fraternal cells of radical insurgents conducting irregular night raids and rapid guerilla mobilization.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Debilitating venom and rapid cell swarming. Fenian raiders strike from the shadows with poisoned dirks, while cell commanders instantly call down guerilla reinforcements.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Head Centre Stephens** (Guerilla commander possessing high tactical mobility and hidden cell command).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Fenian Night Raider` (Melee Squad: Armed with venom-tipped daggers that inflict debilitating poison DOT on enemy infantry).
  2. `Insurgent Cell` (Melee Squad: Rapid guerilla deployment that executes an on-summon trigger: calling down additional commando fighters directly into the combat lane).

### VIII. Chevaliers de la foi (Knights of Faith & Ultra-Royalist Conspirators)
* **Philosophy**: Ultra-royalist aristocratic conspirators sworn to restore absolute monarchial authority and the divine right of kings under the fleur-de-lis.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Unstoppable shock cavalry and inspirational rallying. Their armored cuirassiers shrug off chemical warfare, while royal bannerets inspire nearby friendly troops to strike with supernatural speed.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Ferdinand de Bertier** (Royalist banneret commander possessing superior physical defense and absolute fire immunity).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Royalist Cuirassier` (Vehicle Cavalry: Heavily armored shock cavalry possessing absolute immunity to poison and high tactical movement velocity).
  2. `Royalist Standard Bearer` (Support Squad: Raises the royal standard upon summoning, instantly emitting a rallying cry that grants +30% attack speed to nearby friendly units).

### IX. Ancient Order of Foresters (Woodland Wardens & Druidic Fraternities)
* **Philosophy**: An ancient woodland fraternity sworn to protect the ancient deep woods, mastering botanical toxins and forging alliances with primeval beasts.
* **Tactical Doctrine**: Botanical toxicity and primeval beast swarming. Woodland herbalists throw toxic spore vials, while rugged beastmasters call down ferocious grizzly bears directly into combat lanes.
* **Exclusive Commander**: **Chief Ranger Robin** (Master druidic bowman firing toxic arrows across long distances while commanding woodland beasts).
* **Exclusive Cards**:
  1. `Forester Herbalist` (Ranged Support: Woodland witches hurling toxic spore vials at enemy lines while curing burning and poison debuffs on friendly forces).
  2. `Forester Beastmaster` (Beast Squad: Rugged woodland wardens who execute an on-summon trigger: unleashing ferocious deep-woods grizzly bears into the lane).

## 4. ELEMENTAL & BEHAVIORAL TRIGGER MECHANICS

### Elemental Warfare (Fire & Poison)
1. **Pyromancy & Ignition (`fireAttack`)**: Attacks from Pyre Knights, Carbonari Arsonists, and Greek Fire Flasks instantly ignite targets. Ignited combatants suffer persistent incendiary damage over time (`burnDamagePerSec`) for 6 seconds. Burning combatants evaluate an active probability check each tick (`0.25 * dt`) to spread flames to adjacent units within 30 feet. Combatants possess a baseline 35% probability per second to self-extinguish early.
2. **Toxicity (`poisonAttack`)**: Attacks from Night Raiders, Magus Rosenkreuz, and Forester Herbalists corrupt the enemy blood supply. Poisoned units suffer persistent biological damage over time (`poisonDamagePerSec`) for 5 seconds. Mechanical vehicles (`UnitType.vehicle`) and specialized constructs (`Homunculus Behemoth`) are entirely immune to biological toxins.

### Tactical Targeting Specializations
1. **Anti-Fortification (`TargetingRule.towersOnly`)**: Battering Rams and Masonic Sappers operate under absolute siege discipline. They ignore enemy heroes, infantry screens, and cavalry entirely, marching directly to defensive watchtowers and central bases to deliver devastating structural damage.
2. **Decapitation / Assassination (`TargetingRule.enemyCharacterOnly`)**: Vault Assassins operate under contract assassination protocols. They ignore foot soldiers and defensive installations entirely, aiming their long-range sniper rifles exclusively at enemy Master Commanders and AI Leaders.

### Subversion & Mind Control (`DeathknellEffect.mindControl`)
* Beguilement spells (Hermetic Mesmerist, Astral Hypnosis) temporarily subvert the free will of rival units. When hypnotized, a combatant's allegiance flag (`side`) dynamically inverts to the opposing faction for precisely 12 seconds. Hypnotized units march and attack their former comrades until the hypnotic pendulum duration lapses, whereupon free will is cleanly restored.

### Battlefield Triggers (`Battlehorn` & `Deathknell`)
1. **On-Summon Rallying**: Deploying a Royalist Standard Bearer instantly raises the royal standard, broadcasting an inspirational aura that grants all adjacent friendly units a +30% attack speed bonus for 8 seconds. Deploying Sacred Geometry accelerates friendly tactical locomotion across the entire grid by +40%.
2. **On-Death Retribution**: When a Revolutionary Martyr is vanquished in melee combat, their concealed explosives detonate in a massive 45ft radius blast dealing 150 shrapnel damage to all surrounding enemies. When a Zurich Debt Collector falls in combat, syndicate insurance bonds instantly pay out a +50 CHF financial bounty to the victorious commander.
